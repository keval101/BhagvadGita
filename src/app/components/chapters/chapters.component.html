Chapters

In the Bhagavad Gita, there are a total of 18 chapters, out of which 1st to 6th is talk about how one should do his duties and is called Karma Yoga.
The second set of 6 chapters from chapter 7th to 12th is called as Bhakti Yoga.
The third set of 6 chapters from chapter 13th to 18th is called as Jaana Yoga.
